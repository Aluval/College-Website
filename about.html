GEC, Gudlavalleru! The Gudlavalleru Engineering College, also known as GEC, Gudlavalleru, is a premier engineering institution located in Gudlavalleru, Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 1952, it is known for its strong focus on academic and research excellence. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines such as Engineering, Technology, and Science. It is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) and is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). GEC, Gudlavalleru has a rich history of producing talented engineers and is considered one of the best engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh.
